**FEATURE: AI-DRIVEN ACCOUNTS SYSTEM (UAE STANDARD) – PRIME ERP**

---

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE

Build a **simple, powerful, AI-assisted accounting system** fully integrated with PRIME ERP.

System must:

* Follow UAE VAT standards
* Be easy to use (non-accountant friendly)
* Fully connected with Sales, Procurement, Inventory

---

# 🧾 CORE ACCOUNTING MODULES

### PANELS:

* Accounts Dashboard
* Proforma Invoices
* Tax Invoices
* Delivery Notes
* Expenses
* Payments Received
* Payments Made
* Cheques
* Bank Accounts
* Chart of Accounts
* Journal Entries
* VAT Report

---

# 🤖 AI ROLE (IMPORTANT)

AI must act as:
👉 **Accounting Assistant (not controller)**

AI will:

* Auto-suggest entries
* Auto-fill data from ERP
* Validate VAT compliance
* Reduce manual work

---

# 🔗 ERP INTEGRATION (VERY IMPORTANT)

System must auto-fetch data from:

* Quotation → Invoice
* LPO → Accounts
* Inventory → Cost tracking
* Project → Expense allocation

👉 No duplicate data entry allowed

---

# 🧾 INVOICE SYSTEM (UAE VAT STANDARD)

## TAX INVOICE MUST INCLUDE:

* Invoice Number (Auto)
* Client Name
* TRN Number
* Company TRN
* Item Details
* Quantity
* Unit Price
* VAT % (5%)
* VAT Amount
* Total Amount

---

## 🔹 PAYMENT TERMS

* Must be manual editable textbox
* Must appear on:

  * Proforma Invoice
  * Tax Invoice
