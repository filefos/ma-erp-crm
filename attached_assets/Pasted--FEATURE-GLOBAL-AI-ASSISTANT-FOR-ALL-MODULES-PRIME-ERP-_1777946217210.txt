**FEATURE: GLOBAL AI ASSISTANT FOR ALL MODULES – PRIME ERP**

---

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE

Implement a **powerful AI Assistant in every module** of PRIME ERP to help users perform tasks faster, easier, and with fewer errors.

AI must be:

* Simple to use
* Fast
* Controlled (no independent actions)
* Supportive (assist, not replace user decisions)

---

# 🔒 CORE RULE

* AI must work ONLY inside ERP
* No automatic execution without user confirmation
* All AI suggestions must be editable
* All actions must be logged

---

# 🧠 AI ASSISTANT BEHAVIOR (COMMON FOR ALL MODULES)

In every module:

* Show AI Assistant button
  👉 “Ask AI”

* AI can:

  * Suggest data
  * Auto-fill fields
  * Explain actions
  * Validate entries

---

# 📁 MODULE-WISE AI FUNCTIONS

---

## 📁 CRM (Contacts / Leads)

AI should:

* Suggest company details from name
* Clean duplicate contacts
* Recommend lead priority
* Suggest follow-up dates

---

## 📊 SALES

AI should:

* Suggest quotation items
* Recommend pricing (based on past data)
* Highlight low profit deals
* Suggest next follow-up

---

## 📦 PROCUREMENT

AI should:

* Suggest suppliers
* Recommend best price
* Compare quotations
* Alert high-cost purchases

---

## 📊 INVENTORY

AI should:

* Predict low stock
* Suggest reorder
* Detect dead stock
* Track fast-moving items

---

## 🏗 PROJECTS

AI should:

* Track project progress
* Suggest resource allocation
* Alert delays
* Estimate cost vs actual

---

## 💰 ACCOUNTS

AI should:

* Suggest account entries
* Validate VAT calculations (UAE standard)
* Track pending payments
* Recommend follow-ups

---

## 🏢 ASSETS

AI should:

* Track asset usage
* Suggest maintenance
* Monitor depreciation
* Alert asset issues

---

# 🔗 DATA INTEGRATION

AI must use data from:

* Clients
* Sales
* Inventory
* Procurement
* Accounts

👉 No isolated AI logic

---

# ⚙️ UI REQUIREMENTS

Every module must have:

* AI Button: “Ask AI”
* AI Panel (side popup)
* Quick Suggestions list

---

# 📊 LOGGING SYSTEM

Track:

* User
* Module
* Action
* Date & Time

---

# 🚫 RESTRICTIONS

* AI must NOT delete data
* AI must NOT auto-submit forms
* AI must NOT modify records without approval

---

# 🚀 FINAL OBJECTIVE

Create a **smart assistant across ERP** that:

* Saves time
* Reduces errors
* Improves decisions
* Keeps system simple
